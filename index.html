optimize nutrition while minimize decisions

i used to waste 2 hours every day on thinking about what to eat.

one day, i decided to build app to free me from all decision making fatigue. it took me 5 minutes to input my preferences, goals and i never had to think about what to eat ever since.

a nutritious daily meal plan you can just follow, forever.

    your brain gets a break you know how steve jobs wore the same black shirt every day? same concept but for food. one less thing to think about, more mental space for stuff that actually matters.
    you get back your time no more standing in front of the fridge for 10 minutes. no more scrolling through delivery apps. no more grocery store wandering. you know exactly what you're eating and can just get on with your day.
    checkout all the macros when you eat the same thing daily, you can dial in exactly the right amount of protein, carbs, fats, everything. no guessing if you're eating enough or too much of something.
    it's weirdly liberating sounds boring, but it's actually freeing. like having a uniform for your stomach. you stop thinking about it and just... eat the thing that makes you feel good.

optimize your diet, time and brain power from today

takes like 5 minutes to set up. you tell it what you like and what you're going for, it gives you a plan. if you hate it, whatever, but if you're like me and just want food to be simple, this might be useful.
